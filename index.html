What is Community
Science Museum

Community Science Museum is a place where kids, where they can learn about science in an interesting and fun way. There are interactive exhibits and many hands-on activities available. Children can also celebrate their birthdays and enjoy a mini amusement park there. All of this is done to make learning easier and more enjoyable for kids.
